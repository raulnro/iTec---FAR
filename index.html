FAQ

How can I pay for my order?

We accept payments with bank cards, bank transfers and cash on delivery. For card payments, we use a secure system that protects your data.

  • We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express and bank transfers
  • All card payments are processed through a secure system
  • Cash on delivery is available for all locations
How long does the delivery take?

Standard delivery takes between 2-4 business days. For orders placed before 2 PM, delivery can be processed the same day.

  • Standard delivery: 2-4 business days
  • Express delivery: 24 hours (additional fee)
  • Same day delivery available for orders before 2 PM
What is our return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of receiving the product, if it is not damaged and is in the original packaging.

  • 14-day return period
  • Product must be in original condition
  • Return shipping costs covered by customer
Do you offer a guarantee for products?

Yes, all our products come with a minimum 2-year warranty. This covers manufacturing defects and technical issues.

  • Minimum 2-year warranty on all products
  • Covers manufacturing defects
  • Free repairs or replacements for warranty claims
